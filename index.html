About Blithchron

Welcome to Blithchron. Born in the year 2009, this festival derives it's name from two smaller words "BLITHE" meaning festival and "CHRONOS" meaning time, rendering the complete word to mean "time of festivity". What can be a better way to hit the crux of a festival?

Through the years providing a "one of a kind" experience has been the predicament of the festival and achieving it every year has been our legacy. So carrying the same legacy forwards this time, go through the magical experience of weaving through the streets of mystical India. Experience the country that is a perfect amalgamation of countless cultures, and drown yourselves in its many varied colors.

Once again, IIT Gandhinagar is back with Euphony – a one of a kind solo singing competition in Blithchron’16.
