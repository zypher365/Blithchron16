Overview

Gear up for Unmaad with an amazing journey this 17th of January, 2016 (Sunday). As we rejuvenate ourselves this Unmaad with ‘A Breath of Life’, take this opportunity to explore Bangalore through roads less travelled to places less frequented. Make the most of what this beautiful city has to offer by cracking clues, completing fun and exciting tasks and winning special goodies for each of them, all within the city of Bangalore! So get a two-wheeler and bring along a teammate to participate in the most awaited prelude event ‘Road To Unmaad’ 2016. What’s more? Attractive prizes worth over 50,000/- to be won, with many more surprises in store!

General Instructions
  • THIS IS NOT A RACE! The event is all about having fun and exploring Bangalore. Remember to do that! And guess what, you get exciting prizes for it!
  • Each team MUST have two members and a two wheeler (bike/scooter) with a functioning odometer. Cars are not allowed.
  • Please ensure that you have relevant vehicle documentation. Teams are advised to carry helmets. Team Unmaad will not be responsible for any penalties due to violation of traffic rules.
  • The teams are requested to exercise caution on the road. Speeding is discouraged since time (We repeat!) is not a judging criteria. The organizing team will not take responsibility for any mishaps or vehicle breakdown during the event. The change of vehicle amidst the event is not acceptable and will lead to disqualification.
  • Read point 1 again!
Rules
  • Each team will be given a set of 11 clues corresponding to the relevant pit-stops. The teams will have to locate the pit-stops on their own.
  • In the event of failing to locate a pit-stop, the team can contact an emergency phone number at the IIMB Helpdesk to know the exact location with a penalty in the final score.
  • The reporting time for the start of the event is 9am at IIMB campus. Teams can leave the campus to start visiting pit-stops at 10 am.
  • Teams shall be assigned the first pit-stop to be visited by lottery system. In addition, it is mandatory for teams to cover at least 4 pit-stops (one from each cluster).
  • At each pit-stop, teams need to physically report to the pit-stop in-charge and complete the pit-stop formalities.
  • Please take a photo of your vehicle odometer and show it to the pit-stop in-charge. This is mandatory for your task to begin.
  • At each pit-stop, teams are required to perform the task administered by the pit-stop in-charge to earn points.
  • Each team is free to choose the order of pit-stops, after the first pit-stop allotted by lottery has been covered.
  • Teams will perform the task in the order of arrival at the pit-stop, i.e., on a first come first serve basis. In case of a conflict, the pit-stop in-charge’s decision shall be final and binding.
  • Initial and Final odometer readings will be taken at the IIMB Helpdesk at the beginning and at the end of the event.
  • You will be intelligent enough to understand that speeding and saving time fetched you no extra points. Drive slow and enjoy Bangalore!
